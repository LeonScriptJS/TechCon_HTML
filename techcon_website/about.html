History of TechCon

Ever since the first celebration of the TechCon in the year 1997 at Tafawa Balewa Square, the coming together of genius nigerian minds in the tech world have become a must to encourage innovation and creativity in our youths.

Our Mission and aspiring Goals

We strive daily, aspiring to develop the world of tech in our country Nigeria, especially among our youthsand upcoming entrepreneurs. Our NGO's and support outlet and Hubs accross the nation serve as out arms and legs to reach the and empower our youth with the creativity and ambition to survive and excel in the world of technological advancements and reserach globally. Some of our goals include:

  • Provision of fundings for aspiring entrepreneurs.
  • Creation of fully equiped and easily accessable facilities around the country called Hubs.
  • Consistent hosting of tech events, AMA's with Tech CEO's and bootcamps to impact knowledge and empower our youths.
